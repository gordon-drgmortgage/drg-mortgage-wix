Important education and licensing notes

DRG Mortgage is a mortgage broker, not a direct lender. Loan options, pricing, terms, approvals, and underwriting decisions are provided by independent wholesale lender partners and may vary by borrower profile, property details, loan purpose, documentation, market conditions, and applicable guidelines.

Educational examples, calculators, market commentary, flyers, and social media resources are for general informational purposes only. They are not a commitment to lend, loan approval, lock agreement, rate quote, guarantee of terms, or financial, legal, tax, or investment advice.

Any market-rate information or benchmark commentary shown in DRG educational materials should be viewed as general market context. When third-party sources such as Freddie Mac are cited, those figures are not DRG Mortgage rates and may not reflect the rate, price, loan program, or eligibility available for any specific borrower.
